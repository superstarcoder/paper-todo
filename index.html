The Paper Todo philosophy

Get away from the noise.
Just get things done.

Modern productivity apps have become the very thing they set out to solve. Paper Todo strips it all back. No onboarding flows, no dashboards to configure, no ads, ever. Just you, your tasks, and a tool that gets out of your way.

Everything you actually need

One clean workspace.

Tasks

Capture everything on your plate. Set priorities, deadlines, and categories. Check things off and feel good about it.

Habits

Build routines that stick. Track your streaks, pick which days to repeat, and watch your consistency grow over time.

Logs

Track numbers that matter to you. Weight, hours slept, miles run. Log entries and see your trend at a glance.

Stats

Your personal productivity picture. Completion charts, category breakdowns, habit heatmaps, and streak tracking.

Multiple Views

Switch between List, Grid, and Stats. The weekly grid view lets you plan your days by category and drag tasks around.

Themes

Light, Dark, or Earth mode, where the sky actually follows the real sun wherever you are in the world.
