Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026
☁

The short version: Paper Todo does not collect your personal data, does not run ads, and does not sell anything about you to anyone. Your tasks, habits, and logs live on your own device.

What we store and where

All of your workspace data (tasks, habits, trackers, categories, and settings) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage under the key tracker_v1. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server we operate.

Clearing your browser data or localStorage will permanently delete your workspace. We recommend using the CSV export (Settings menu) to keep a backup.

Third-party services

Paper Todo loads a small number of external resources to function. Here is exactly what they are and what they may see:

Service Purpose What they may receive
Google Fonts Loads Instrument Serif and DM Sans typefaces Your IP address and browser headers, per Google's standard CDN logging. Google's privacy policy
ZenQuotes API Fetches a random motivational quote shown in the app Your IP address via the allorigins.win CORS proxy. No personal data is sent. The quote fetch is optional and fails silently if unavailable.

No analytics, no tracking pixels, no advertising SDKs, no session recording. That's the complete list.

Cookies

Paper Todo does not use cookies. The only browser storage used is localStorage, which is not transmitted with requests and is not accessible to third parties.

Your data rights

Because we hold no personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on your behalf. Your data is already entirely yours:

  • To export: use the CSV export option in the Settings menu inside the app.
  • To delete: clear localStorage in your browser's developer tools or site settings.
  • To transfer: export as CSV and import it wherever you like.

Future changes

We may introduce optional account-based features in the future (such as cross-device sync). These will always be opt-in. If and when that happens, this policy will be updated to clearly describe what additional data is collected, how it is stored, and how you can remove it. We will never collect data silently.
